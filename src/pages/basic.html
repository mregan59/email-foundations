Rejection feels awful. Nobody enjoys feeling like there is something wrong with yourself or that you aren't good enough.

Dear *|FNAME|*,

Often, that's exactly how rejection leaves us feeling.

But, as awful as it is, rejection is unavoidable.

Not everybody will want to date you. Some of your friendships will end. Jobs will turn you down. You won't always get your way.

But, that's okay.

Don’t let those rejections make you believe the lie that you’re fundamentally flawed or unworthy.

If you struggle with doing that, remember this:

You’re so worthy that Jesus died for you.

And, “If God is for us, who can be against us?” (Romans 8:31).

You should remind yourself of those realities because they are just as real as the rejections that you will inevitably experience.
